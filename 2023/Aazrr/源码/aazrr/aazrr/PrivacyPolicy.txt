Privacy Policy for Aazrr

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to Aazrr! Your privacy is critically important to us. We have built this application with a fundamental commitment to user privacy.

1. No Data Collection
Aazrr operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information, usage data, or device analytics. There are no tracking scripts, analytics SDKs, or advertising networks integrated into this app.

2. Local Storage Only
All your actions within the app, including generated posters, selected portraits, and customized quotes, are processed and stored locally on your device. When you save a poster, it is saved directly to your local Photo Library using standard system APIs.

3. No Accounts Required
We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any contact information (such as email or phone number) to use any feature of this application.

4. Camera & Photos Access
The app requires access to your Photo Library exclusively for the purpose of saving the posters you create. We do not read or upload your existing photos.

5. Third-Party Links
This application does not contain links to third-party websites or services. Everything is self-contained.

6. Changes to This Policy
If we make changes to this policy, we will update the internal document. However, since we do not collect data, any changes will only reinforce this commitment.

Contact: Because we do not collect your information, we cannot be reached through the app. For App Store related inquiries, please refer to the support link in the App Store.
